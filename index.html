Interests

During my high school years, I was very fascinated with novels and comics about sci-fi and fantasy, which gave me a lot of thoughts about the various possibilities of the world. At this time, I had a question: "What kind of rules do we people follow?"
One day I observed that the computer has input and output characteristics, which gave me an inspiration and method to find out the rules of human behavior.
My current interest is to find the similarities between computers and human beings when processing messages , finally realizing real artificial intelligence with computers.

Prospects

My future goal is to design a method for reading the contextual message in which the utterance is located, and then to decode the control signal and the actual content in the utterance text, and construct a program for each text in the utterance according to the sentence context.
